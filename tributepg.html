Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna
  • Inspiring teacher and author
